King Von

Full Name: Dayvon Daquan Bennett (August 9, 1994 – November 6, 2020) was an American rapper from Chicago, Illinois.

He was an affiliate of the Black Disciples gang and was considered a preeminent figure in the drill genre.

Bennett's 2018 single, "Crazy Story", and his 2020 single, "Took Her to the O", both received triple platinum certifications by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). The latter peaked within the top 50 of the Billboard Hot 100 and preceded his debut studio album Welcome to O'Block (2020), which peaked at number five on the Billboard 200.

He was signed to Lil Durk's record label, Only the Family.

On November 6, 2020, Bennett was shot and killed at the age of 26.
